Which Tier Is Right for You?

The Standard Commission tier is well-suited for clients who prefer a lower upfront commitment, with a 25% commission applied to the total proceeds of the sale. The Full-Service tier is ideal for clients who anticipate a high-volume sale and wish to maximize their take-home earnings with a single flat fee and no commission.

🏠 What Happens After the Sale?

At the conclusion of the event, you decide what happens next. Keep any remaining items you wish to retain, or have us remove the remaining inventory completely free of charge.

Peace of Mind

Weather Policy

We offer optional weather insurance to protect your investment in case of severe weather conditions.

☑️ With Weather Insurance $125

In the event of severe weather, you will receive your full service fee refunded, or the opportunity to reschedule to a different day at no additional cost. Severe weather includes: wind over 30mph, rain over 0.7in for the day, thunder/lightning, and any other severe and unusual weather.

⚠️ Without Weather Insurance

If the sale is canceled due to severe weather without insurance coverage, a 30% refund of the flat service fee will be issued. We strongly recommend weather insurance for full peace of mind going into your event.
